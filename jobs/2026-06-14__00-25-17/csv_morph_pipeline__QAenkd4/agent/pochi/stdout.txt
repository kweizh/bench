## Key Design Decisions

- **Explicit morph pipes**: `age` uses `string.integer.parse |> 0 <= number <= 150` and `signupAt` uses `string.date.iso.parse` — both reach `Date`/`number` via ArkType morphs, not hand-rolled conversions
- **CSV parsing as user-defined morph**: The `string → Array<raw record>` transformation is a morph within the ArkType pipeline, not pre-processing
- **Error handling**: Structural errors (empty input, wrong header, wrong column count) throw from the morph and are caught in the CLI; field constraint errors surface through ArkType's `ArkErrors.summary`
