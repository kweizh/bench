## What was implemented

**`main.go`** — Added a shared event hook handler `computeReadingStats` that:
- Extracts the `content` field from the record
- Splits it on whitespace using `strings.Fields` to count words
- Computes `reading_time_minutes` as `ceil(word_count / 200)` (0 words → 0 minutes)
- Overwrites both `word_count` and `reading_time_minutes` on the record
- Calls `e.Next()` so the request chain continues and the record is persisted

The hook is registered on both `OnRecordCreateRequest("articles")` and `OnRecordUpdateRequest("articles")`.
