# Chained Checkpoint Restoration with Tigris Agent Kit ## Background Production AI pipelines mutate shared buckets on every step. When a mutation needs to be audited or rolled back, the typical workflow is: snapshot the bucket before the risky write, perform the write, then restore the snapshot into an isolated fork for inspection.
