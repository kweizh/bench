## How the URL sync works

- `validateSearch` on the `/search` route coerces raw query-string values into `{ q, category, minPrice, maxPrice }` with correct types (`string` / `number`).
- `useSearch({ from: '/search' })` reads the typed, validated params — so inputs are always populated from the URL on load.
- Every `onChange` calls `navigate({ search: (prev) => ({ ...prev, ...patch }), replace: true })`, updating the URL immediately without adding a history entry per keystroke.
- A live `<pre>` preview at the bottom of the page shows the current parsed params for easy verification.
