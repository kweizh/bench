## How it works

| File | Purpose |
|---|---|
| `mockApi.ts` | `fetchFeedPage(cursor)` returns 5 items per page and a `nextCursor` (or `null` on the last page). 15 items across 3 pages are pre-seeded. |
| `Feed.tsx` | `useInfiniteQuery` with `initialPageParam: 1` and `getNextPageParam` reading `lastPage.nextCursor`. All accumulated pages are rendered flat in a `<ul>`. |
| `Feed.tsx` | **"Load More"** button is shown while `hasNextPage` is true; it calls `fetchNextPage()` and is disabled during loading. Once all pages are consumed it shows "You've reached the end of the feed." |
