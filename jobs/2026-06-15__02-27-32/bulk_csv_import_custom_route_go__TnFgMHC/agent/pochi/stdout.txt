   | Check | Behaviour |
   |---|---|

   | Any errors collected | 400 `{"inserted":0,"errors":[{"row":<N>,"reason":"..."},...]}` |
   | All rows valid | single `RunInTransaction` insert; 200 `{"inserted":<N>,"errors":[]}` |
