# Understanding AI Agents

AI agents are transforming the landscape of knowledge work by leveraging the capabilities of large language models (LLMs). These autonomous software systems are designed to execute complex tasks by planning multi-step workflows, utilizing various tools, and reflecting on their outputs to improve performance. 

## The Power of Planning and Tool Use

The effectiveness of AI agents lies in their ability to combine planning and tool use. By orchestrating multiple actions and integrating external tools, these agents can automate processes that traditionally required human intervention. This not only increases efficiency but also allows organizations to focus on higher-level strategic initiatives while the agents handle routine tasks.

## Innovations in AI Agents

Alchemyst AI is at the forefront of this innovation, providing AI agents with persistent memory and retrieval-augmented generation (RAG) capabilities. This means that agents can remember past interactions and utilize them to inform future decisions, enhancing their overall effectiveness. With features like document chunking, embedding generation, and similarity search, Alchemyst AI is setting a new standard for what autonomous agents can achieve in the realm of knowledge work.
