## Key Design Decisions

- **Headless backend**: Uses `com.badlogic.gdx.backends.headless.HeadlessApplication` with `updatesPerSecond = 0` for maximum throughput. The constructor blocks on the calling thread until `Gdx.app.exit()` is called, so no explicit thread join is needed.
- **Fixed timestep**: Each `render()` call advances exactly one tick with `delta = 1.0`. `Gdx.graphics.getDeltaTime()` is never used for physics.
- **Pool recycling**: `Pool<Projectile>` with `obtain()`/`free()`. `pool.getFree()` is queried at the end for the summary.
- **Integer output**: All Vector2 components are cast with `(int)` for printing.
- **Deterministic ordering**: Active projectiles and grounded projectiles are sorted by spawn-id before output.
- **`done` flag**: Prevents re-entry into `render()` after the simulation completes, since the headless backend may invoke `render()` once more after `exit()`.
