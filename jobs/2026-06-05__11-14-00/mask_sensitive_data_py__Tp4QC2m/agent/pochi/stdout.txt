## How `run.py` works

**PII masking** – a `mask(*, data, **kwargs)` function (matching the `MaskFunction` protocol) is registered with the Langfuse client. It recurses into dicts and lists and applies three regexes to every string:
- `_EMAIL_RE` → `[REDACTED EMAIL]`
- `_PHONE_RE` (handles `-`, `.`, space separators) → `[REDACTED PHONE]`
- `_CC_RE` (13–19 digits, optional space/dash groups) → `[REDACTED CREDIT CARD]`

**Trace creation** – uses `start_as_current_observation(as_type="span")` for the root span (named `mask-demo-<ZEALT_RUN_ID>`) with PII-laden `input`/`output`, then nests a child `as_type="generation"` observation (`llm-call`) with its own PII payloads. `langfuse.flush()` ensures delivery before the process exits. The trace ID is captured from `root_span.trace_id` and written as `Trace ID: <id>` to `output.log`.
