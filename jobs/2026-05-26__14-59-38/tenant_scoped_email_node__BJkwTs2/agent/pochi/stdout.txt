1. **Set tenant** (`knock.tenants.set`) — creates/upserts `tenant-${RUN_ID}` with:
   - `name`: `Tenant ${RUN_ID}` (contains the run ID)
   - `settings.branding.primary_color`: `#4F46E5`
   - `app_name`: `MyApp-${RUN_ID}` (custom top-level property)

2. **Upsert workflow** (`mgmt.workflows.upsert`) — creates `tenant-welcome-${RUN_ID}` in the `development` environment with a single `mailtrap` channel step:

   - HTML body references both `{{ recipient.name }}` and `{{ tenant.name }}`
   - `channel_overrides.from_address`: `sender-${RUN_ID}@${MAILTRAP_DOMAIN}`
   - Committed immediately via `commit: true`

3. **Activate workflow** (`mgmt.workflows.activate`) — activates `tenant-welcome-${RUN_ID}` in `development` with `status: true`

4. **Trigger workflow** (`knock.workflows.trigger`) — fires the workflow with:
   - `tenant: tenant-${RUN_ID}`
   - Single inline recipient: `id=user-${RUN_ID}`, `email=${GMAIL_USER_NAME}+receiver-${RUN_ID}@gmail.com`

5. **Write log file** at `/home/user/tenant_task/output.log` with the 4 required lines.
