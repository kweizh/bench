# Wide-to-Long Stocks Multi-Line Chart with Vega-Altair `transform_fold` ## Background A common analytics task is to plot several time series whose values live in separate columns (wide form) as a single multi-line chart. Vega-Altair exposes the `transform_fold` transform that reshapes wide-form data into long form directly inside the chart specification, so you do not have to pre-melt the DataFrame in pandas.
